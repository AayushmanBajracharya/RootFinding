Secant Method Root Finding

About the Secant Method

The secant method is an iterative root-finding algorithm that uses two initial guesses to approximate the derivative. It's faster than bisection but may not always converge. The formula is: x_{n+1} = x_n - f(x_n) * (x_n - x_{n-1}) / (f(x_n) - f(x_{n-1}))
